Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 1, 2026

1. The Short Version

StickToGif is a local-first application. We do not upload, process, or store your videos, GIFs, or images on our servers. All tracking, decoding, and encoding happens entirely on your device.

2. Data Collection

We do not require user accounts. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII). Any media you process with StickToGif remains in your device's memory and is discarded when you refresh the page.

As part of standard web hosting operations (via GitHub Pages), connection metadata such as IP addresses may be logged by infrastructural partners to prevent abuse.

3. Third-Party Services

Our application uses standard Native Sharing APIs to hand off your finished files to the messenger or social application of your choice. StickToGif has no visibility into what you do with the files post-export.

4. Contact

If you have any questions regarding how your data is handled, please contact us at hi@chapiware.com.
