Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes. StickToGif started as an open-source experiment in porting OpenCV tracking algorithms to the browser. There are no paywalls, accounts, or hidden "Premium" exports.

How can it be private without a server?

Thanks to Modern Web Standards (WebAssembly and Web Workers), the heavy math required to analyze the video frames utilizes your device's hardware directly. No frame of your video is ever transmitted to a server — this is a technical fact about how the app is built, not just a policy promise.

What formats are supported?

You can import Animated GIFs, MP4s, or MOVs (iPhone video) directly from your device. The application outputs optimized Animated WebPs or standard GIFs.

Can I use my own images?

Absolutely. You can upload any image from your device to use as a tracker, or choose from our built-in emoji presets. You can also add text captions or blur effects that follow the motion.
