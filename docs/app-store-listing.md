# StickToGif App Store Metadata

This document contains the exact ASO-optimized copy for both Google Play and Apple App Store submissions, as finalized in the marketing and positioning analysis. You can copy and paste these directly into the App Store Connect and Google Play Console.

---

## Google Play Store

**App Title (max 50 chars)**:
`StickToGif — GIF Sticker Tracker`

**Short Description (max 80 chars)**:
`Pin stickers, text & blur onto moving subjects in GIFs. No uploads. Free.`

**Full Description**:

StickToGif pins tracked stickers, text overlays, or blur effects onto moving subjects in GIFs and short video clips — running entirely on your device.

No cloud processing. No file uploads. No account required. No watermarks.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

1. Import a GIF or MP4 from your device
2. Tap the thing you want to track
3. Hit Track — the engine follows it frame by frame
4. Choose a sticker, add text, or blur the subject
5. Save the finished GIF to your on-device library or share it instantly

── WHAT YOU CAN DO ──

🕶️ STICKER TRACKING
Upload any image or pick from built-in emoji presets. The sticker follows your subject through every frame automatically.

✍️ TEXT TRACKING
Add a caption or label that sticks to a moving person, object, or animal as it moves across the frame.

🔲 FACE & OBJECT BLUR
Blur a moving face, license plate, or screen before sharing — no timeline, no keyframes.

── WHY STICKTOGIF ──

Every alternative either uploads your video to a remote server, charges a monthly subscription, stamps a watermark on your export, or requires an account just to make one GIF.

StickToGif does none of these. The tracking engine runs entirely inside the app. Your files stay on your device, can be saved into your local creations library, and can be shared when you choose. Nothing is transmitted.

── PERFECT FOR ──

- Making reaction GIFs for Discord, Telegram, or WhatsApp
- Blurring faces or sensitive data before sharing clips
- Adding captions that follow moving subjects in memes
- Processing product demo GIFs without cloud rendering delays
- Privacy-conscious users who don't want to upload personal clips

── SUPPORTED FORMATS ──

Input: Animated GIF, MP4 (up to 15s, 30MB)
Output: Animated GIF

── TECHNICAL NOTES ──

Tracking uses LK optical flow (OpenCV compiled to WebAssembly) with template matching assist for low-confidence frames. Processing runs in a background thread. Mobile builds save exports locally on-device and can re-share them through native system sheets.

Free. Open source. No backend.

## Apple App Store

**App Name (max 30 chars)**:
`StickToGif: GIF Sticker Tracker`

**Subtitle (max 30 chars)**:
`Auto-Track & Pin Stickers`

*Note: You originally saw "Auto-Track & Pin Stickers on Any GIF" but that is 35 characters which exceeds Apple's 30 character limit for Subtitles. It has been reduced slightly to fit perfectly!*

**Keywords (max 100 chars, comma-separated)**:
`gif editor,sticker tracker,meme maker,blur face,gif overlay,motion track,gif maker,emoji sticker,no upload,gif blur`
*(Note: Do not add spaces after commas. Apple's system does not read them correctly if spaces are included)*

**Promotional Text (max 170 chars)**:
`No uploads. No account. No watermarks. Save and share GIFs on-device.`

**Description**:

StickToGif pins tracked stickers, text, and blur effects onto moving subjects in animated GIFs, MP4s, and iPhone videos — and everything runs directly on your device.

Your videos are never uploaded anywhere.

WHAT IT DOES

Tap the thing you want to track. StickToGif follows it across every frame using optical flow tracking, then attaches a sticker, text caption, or mosaic blur that moves with it automatically. Export as an animated GIF, save it into My Creations on your device, and share it whenever you want.

FEATURES

- Motion-tracked sticker overlays
- Text that follows a moving subject
- Blur or mosaic effect for faces, screens, or plates
- Upload your own images as stickers
- Built-in emoji preset stickers
- GIF, MP4, and MOV input
- Animated GIF export
- My Creations local library for saved exports
- Native iOS share sheet — share directly from saved creations or after export
- No account, no subscription, no watermarks

COMPLETELY PRIVATE

The tracking engine runs on-device using WebAssembly. Your files are processed locally, saved locally, and only shared when you explicitly choose to do so. No frame of your video is ever transmitted to a server — this is a technical fact about how the app is built, not a policy promise.

PERFECT FOR

Making reaction GIFs with tracked stickers for Discord or iMessage. Blurring a face before sharing a clip from your camera roll. Adding a caption that follows someone through a meme. Processing a product demo clip without waiting for cloud rendering.

SUPPORTED FORMATS

Input: GIF, MP4, MOV (iPhone video), up to 15 seconds
Output: Animated GIF

Free. No in-app purchases. No subscription.
